Why these are irreducible. P.1 provides re-entry (endomorphism). P.2 provides non-trivial domain (|D| ≥ 2). Neither generates the other. No later paper introduces a hidden primitive — the generation taxonomy (Paper T-GOV §1) classifies every framework object’s origin, and all trace to P.1 + P.2 through the bridge chain.

What they generate. Everything. The entire framework is SRD at increasing tower depth through three projection faces (§2 below). The organizing principle is R(R) = R: self-action stabilizes.
